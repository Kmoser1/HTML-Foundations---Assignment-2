Summary of Cylinders

Cylinders spanned a half-century of technological development in sound recording. As documents of American cultural history and musical style,

cylinders serve as an audible witness to the sounds and songs through which typical audiences first encountered the recorded human voice."

Source: Cylinder History, UCSB.
